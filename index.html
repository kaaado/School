University Overview
Founded in 1986, Université Mustapha Stambouli de Mascara is a non-profit public higher-education institution located in the urban setting of the city of Mascara (population range of 50,000-249,999 inhabitants). Officially recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research of Algeria,Univ Mascara is a large (uniRank enrollment range: 20,000-24,999 students) .
